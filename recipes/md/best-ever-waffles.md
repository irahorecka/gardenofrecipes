# Best Ever Waffles

May also be used as pancake batter.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp oil
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs
2. Add milk and oil
3. Sift flour, salt, sugar, and baking powder
4. Mix the milk mixture into the dry ingredients
5. Bake in a greased waffle iron
