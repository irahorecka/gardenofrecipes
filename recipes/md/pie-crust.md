# Pie Crust

Very good from Dorothy. Makes 2 9-inch crusts.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups sifted flour
- 2/3 cup shortening
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 4 to 5 tbsp cold water

## Instructions

1. Mix together flour, shortening, and salt
2. Add 4 to 5 tablespoons cold water
3. Chill dough
4. Roll between waxed paper
