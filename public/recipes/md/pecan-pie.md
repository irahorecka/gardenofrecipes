# Pecan Pie

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs, beaten
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 T lemon juice
- 1 T cornstarch
- 1 cup white Karo
- 1 tsp melted butter or oleo
- dash of salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Beat all ingredients together
2. Add chopped pecans
3. Pour into a crust
4. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes
5. Test with a stainless steel knife inserted into the centerâ€”if it comes out clean, the pie is done
6. Cool on wire rack
