# Cornmeal Pie II

From San Angelo Paper

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs, beaten
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1/2 cup melted oleo
- 1/4 cup milk
- Grated rind and juice of one lemon
- 3 tbsp cornmeal

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and flour
2. Add remaining ingredients and mix well
3. Pour into a 9-inch pie shell
4. Bake at 275 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes
